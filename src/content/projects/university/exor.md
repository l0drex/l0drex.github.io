We developed a 3D game in Unity where you can
walk around an operating room,
talk to the people working there,
see descriptions of various robots and devices
and play two minigames.

![](@assets/projects/exor.png)

The project was developed in a team of 4 people using Unity and C# scripts.
We worked together with [6G life](https://6g-life.de/) and implemented their requirements.
We targeted pupils at an age of 8 to 12 years old.
The final piece would be a wall-mounted touch screen.

The exhibition piece will be part of the [MS Wissenschaft tour 2026](https://ms-wissenschaft.de/en/).
